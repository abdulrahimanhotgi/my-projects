School

InfoStack Primary School is also known as junior school, grade school, or elementary school. It is for children who are 4-11 years old. This school comes after preschool​,​ and before secondary school. Education occurs in a single phase that designs fundamental skills such as writing, learning, and reading. Understand and digitize school operations with Teachmint and its features like the performance management for efficient school management. The primary school delivers the first education to the student, creating a strong foundation for learning and improving their educational skills. According to the ISCED definition, primary education naturally starts from 5 years lasts to 8 years of age, and it develops learning among students so that they can understand subjects, quickly write, and learn.​ It plays a pivotal part in character development.
